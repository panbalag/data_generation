Write a detailed game walkthrough guide for the game Doom (2016)

Doom is a first-person shooter game developed by id Software and published by Bethesda Softworks. It was released on May 13, 2016 for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One. The game takes place in a research facility on Mars, where a demonic invasion has occurred, and the player must fight to survive and stop the invasion.

Here is a detailed walkthrough guide for Doom:

Chapter 1: UAC Research Facility on Mars

* Start the game and move through the opening cutscene.
* Once you gain control of the player, move forward and enter the UAC Research Facility on Mars.
* As you move through the facility, you will encounter various enemies, including imps and demons. Use your fists and the combat shotgun to defeat them.
* Continue moving through the facility until you reach the first checkpoint.
* At the checkpoint, you will encounter a cyberdemon. Defeat it using your weapons and continue moving through the facility.

Chapter 2: The Halls of Agony

* After the checkpoint, you will enter the Halls of Agony. This area is filled with enemies, including zombies and pain elementals. Use your weapons to defeat them.
* As you move through the area, you will encounter a demonic priest. Defeat him to progress.
* Continue moving through the area until you reach the next checkpoint.

Chapter 3: The Arena

* At the checkpoint, you will enter the Arena. This area is filled with enemies, including revenants and mancubi. Use your weapons to defeat them.
* As you move through the area, you will encounter a cyberdemon. Defeat it to progress.
* Continue moving through the area until you reach the next checkpoint.

Chapter 4: The Waste Processing Plant

* At the checkpoint, you will enter the Waste Processing Plant. This area is filled with enemies, including demonic imps and hell knights. Use your weapons to defeat them.
* As you move through the area, you will encounter a baron of Hell. Defeat him to progress.
* Continue moving through the area until you reach the next checkpoint.

Chapter 5: The Toxic Sewers

* At the checkpoint, you will enter the Toxic Sewers. This area is filled with enemies, including zombies and demonic imps. Use your weapons to defeat them.
* As you move through the area, you will encounter a demonic priest. Defeat him to progress.
* Continue moving through the area until you reach the next checkpoint.

Chapter 6: The Foundry

* At the checkpoint, you will enter the Foundry. This area is filled with enemies, including mancubi and pain elementals. Use your weapons to defeat them.
* As you move through the area, you will encounter a cyberdemon. Defeat it to progress.
* Continue moving through the area until you reach the final boss fight.

Chapter 7: The Final Battle

* In the final battle, you will face the Hell Guards, who are powerful demons that must be defeated to win the game. Use all of your weapons and abilities to defeat them.
* After defeating the Hell Guards, the game will end and you will be given a final score based on your performance.

Overall, Doom is a fast-paced and action-packed game that requires quick reflexes and strategic thinking to defeat the enemies and progress through the levels. With this walkthrough guide, you should be able to complete the game and enjoy all of the intense action and excitement that it has to offer.
