* Verify that the power button is functioning correctly by pressing it firmly for a few seconds.
* Try using a different power cord or wall outlet to rule out any issues with the current setup.
* Check the fuse in the power cord for any signs of blown fuses or damage.
2. Unexpected shutdowns: The C64 may suddenly shut down during gameplay or while running applications.
