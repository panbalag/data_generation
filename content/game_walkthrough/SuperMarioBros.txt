Write a detailed game walkthrough guide for the game Super Mario Bros. 3.

Super Mario Bros. 3 is a classic platformer game that was released in 1988 for the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) console. The game is the third installment in the Super Mario series and is widely considered one of the greatest video games of all time. In this walkthrough guide, we will provide a detailed overview of the game's story, gameplay, and all the power-ups and secrets that Mario can collect.

Story:

The game's story follows Mario and Luigi as they try to rescue Princess Peach from the clutches of the evil Bowser. Bowser has stolen the Princess and taken her to his castle, and it's up to Mario and Luigi to rescue her. The game is divided into eight worlds, each with four levels, and each level has a different theme, such as desert, sea, sky, and so on.

Gameplay:

Super Mario Bros. 3 is a platformer game that requires players to navigate through levels, collecting power-ups and coins while avoiding enemies. The game features a variety of power-ups, such as mushrooms, fire flowers, and stars, that give Mario and Luigi new abilities and powers.

Power-ups:

* Mushrooms: Give Mario and Luigi an extra life and make them bigger and stronger.
* Fire Flowers: Allow Mario and Luigi to shoot fireballs at enemies.
* Stars: Make Mario and Luigi invincible for a short period of time.

Secrets:

* Warp Pipes: These pipes allow Mario and Luigi to travel to different worlds and levels.
* Secret Warp Pipes: These pipes can be found hidden in certain levels and allow players to access secret levels and worlds.
* Power-up Mushrooms: These mushrooms contain power-ups such as fire flowers and stars.
