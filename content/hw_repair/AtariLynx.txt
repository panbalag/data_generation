4. Button Issues: The Atari Lynx controller has several buttons that can wear out over time. If a button is not functioning properly, try cleaning it with a soft cloth and a mild cleaning solution. If the issue persists, you may need to replace the button.
5. Charging Issues: The Atari Lynx charging port can become dirty or damaged, preventing the console from charging properly. Clean the charging port with a soft cloth and a mild cleaning solution to resolve any charging issues.
