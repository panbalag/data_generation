Write a comprehensive buying guide for the gaming system Nintendo 64.

The Nintendo 64 (N64) is a classic gaming console that was released in the mid-1990s and is still popular among retro gamers today. If you're looking to buy an N64, here's a comprehensive buying guide to help you make the right purchase:

1. Console:
	* The N64 console is the heart of the system, and there are a few different versions available. The most common ones are the original N64, the N64 Console with Expansion Pak, and the N64 Console with 4MB RAM Expansion Pak. The main difference between these versions is the amount of RAM and the presence of an Expansion Pak, which allows for more advanced graphics and gameplay.
	* When buying an N64 console, make sure it comes with all the necessary cables, including the power cord, AV cables, and controller ports.
2. Controllers:
	* The N64 controller is one of the most iconic gaming controllers of all time. It has an unique design with an analog stick, directional pad, and face buttons.
	* There are two types of N64 controllers: the original N64 Controller and the N64 Controller with Analog Stick. The latter has an analog stick that allows for more precise movement and control in games that support it.
	* If you're looking to buy additional controllers, make sure they are compatible with your N64 console and that they have the same features as the original controllers.
3. Games:
	* The N64 has a wide variety of games available, including popular titles like Super Mario 64, The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time, GoldenEye 007, and Banjo-Kazooie.
	* When buying N64 games, make sure they are authentic and in good condition. Some games may have scratches or other damage that can affect gameplay, so be sure to inspect the game before purchasing it.
	* You can also look for games that have been re-released in special editions or that have been enhanced with new features.
4. Accessories:
	* The N64 has a number of accessories available that can enhance your gaming experience. Some popular accessories include:
		+ Expansion Pak: This add-on increases the console's RAM from 4MB to 8MB, allowing for more advanced graphics and gameplay.
		+ Transfer Pak: This device allows you to transfer data from one N64 game to another.
		+ N64 Memory Pak: This accessory allows you to save your game progress directly to the cartridge, so you don't have to keep inserting the game into the console to save your progress.
		+ N64 controller pack: This accessory includes additional controllers and a carrying case for easy storage and transport.
5. Online marketplaces:
	* When buying an N64, you can find them on online marketplaces like Amazon, eBay, and Craigslist.
	* Be sure to read reviews and check the seller's ratings before making a purchase to ensure that you're getting a legitimate product.
6. Local game stores:
	* You can also find N64 consoles and games at local game stores, such as GameStop or Best Buy.
	* These stores may have a wider selection of games and accessories, and you can get hands-on experience with the console before making a purchase.
7. Price:
	* The price of an N64 can vary depending on the version, condition, and location.
	* On average, you can expect to pay between $100 and $300 for a complete N64 console with games and accessories.

In conclusion, buying an N64 can be a fun and rewarding experience, but it's important to do your research and make sure you're getting a legitimate product. With this comprehensive buying guide, you'll be well on your way to enjoying all the classic games and nostalgic fun that the N64 has to offer.
