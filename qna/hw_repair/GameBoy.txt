Question: How do I repair the Game Boy?
Answer: To repair the Game Boy, you will need to remove the battery and any cartridges from the console, use a screwdriver to remove the screws on the back of the console, carefully pry the shell open with a plastic spudger or similar tool, gently remove the front bezel and the screen from the console, insert the new screen into the console and secure it with screws, reattach the shell and the front bezel, replace the screws and any other components that were removed, and finally, replace the battery if needed.
