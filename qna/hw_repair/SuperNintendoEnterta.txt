Question: How do I clean the SNES console?
Answer: Use a soft brush or cloth to gently clean the SNES console, paying special attention to any vents or grills that may be clogged.

Question: What are some maintenance tips for the SNES?
Answer: Some maintenance tips for the SNES include keeping it away from direct sunlight, storing it in a cool, dry place, avoiding dropping or bumping it, unplugging it when not in use, keeping it away from pets and children, and regularly checking for faulty or damaged components.
