Question: What is the game Sonic the Hedgehog (2006) for the Xbox 360?
Answer: Sonic the Hedgehog (2006) is a platformer game developed by Sonic Team and published by Sega. The game was released for the Xbox 360, PlayStation 3, and Windows. The game is a reboot of the classic Sonic the Hedgehog series and features a new story, characters, and gameplay mechanics. The game follows the story of Sonic, a blue hedgehog, and his friends, Tails and Knuckles, as they try to stop the evil Dr. Eggman from taking over the world.
