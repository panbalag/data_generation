Question: What is the story of Donkey Kong Country 2: Diddy's Kong Quest?
Answer: The game starts with Diddy Kong, a young monkey, and his friend Dixie Kong, a pink monkey, setting out to rescue Donkey Kong from the evil King K. Rool and his Kremlings. The two friends must journey through the jungle, swamps, and mountains to reach King K. Rool's castle and defeat him.
