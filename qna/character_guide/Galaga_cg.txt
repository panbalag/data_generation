Question: How to play Galaga?
Answer: Here is a detailed walkthrough guide for Galaga:
 1. Select the Galaga game.
 2. Choose the number of lives you want.
 3. Press the start button to begin.
 4. Use the joystick to move your ship.
 5. Press the fire button to shoot.
 6. Avoid the alien enemies and collect power-ups.
 7. Defeat the alien enemies to advance to the next level.
 8. Complete the game by defeating all the alien enemies.
 9. Enjoy the game!
 10. Have fun!
