Question: What are the features of the Sony PlayStation 4?
Answer: The Sony PlayStation 4 (PS4) features a powerful AMD Jaguar 8-core processor, 8 GB of GDDR5 RAM, a 500 GB hard drive, and a powerful graphics processing unit (GPU). It supports 4K resolution, is compatible with the PlayStation VR headset, and supports streaming of games and videos using the PlayStation Now service. The PS4 also features a social interface that allows players to connect with friends, join groups, and share gameplay highlights.
