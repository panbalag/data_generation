Question: What are the key features and specifications of the Sega Genesis Mini?
Answer: The Sega Genesis Mini is a miniature version of the original Sega Genesis console, which was released in the early 1990s. It comes with a compact design and built-in games, making it a great option for retro gaming enthusiasts who want to play classic games without the bulk of the original console. Here are some of the key features and specifications of the Sega Genesis Mini: * Compact design: The Sega Genesis Mini is about 50% smaller than the original Sega Genesis console, making it easy to place in a small space. * Built-in games: The console comes with 42 built-in games, including popular titles like Sonic the Hedgehog, Streets of Rage, and Phantasy Star. * HDMI output: The Sega Genesis Mini has an HDMI output, which allows you to connect the console to a modern TV for crystal-clear graphics and sound. * Two controllers: The console comes with two wired controllers, which are compatible with the original Sega Genesis games. * Power supply: The Sega Genesis Mini requires a power supply, which is included in the box.
