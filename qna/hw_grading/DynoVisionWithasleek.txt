Question: How is the condition of the DynoVision console graded?
Answer: The condition of the DynoVision console is graded according to the following criteria: * Physical Condition: The console is graded based on the cosmetic damage, signs of wear and tear, loose or damaged connectors, and any cracks or breaks in the plastic casing. * Functional Condition: The console is powered on and tested for functionality, game compatibility and performance, sound and graphics quality, and controller responsiveness and functionality. * Internal Conditions: The console is cleaned and any internal components are checked for dust buildup, corrosion, and any other issues. * Boxed Accessories: The console is graded based on the presence of any boxed accessories such as manuals and documentation, game cartridges or discs, controllers, power cord, and any other included accessories.
