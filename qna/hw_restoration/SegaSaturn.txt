Question: What hardware can be upgraded in the Sega Saturn?
Answer: The Sega Saturn's hardware can be upgraded in several ways. Some common hardware upgrades include upgrading the RAM, replacing worn-out cartridge slots, cleaning and replacing connectors, and replacing the power supply.

Question: What are some common troubleshooting steps for the Sega Saturn?
Answer: Some common troubleshooting steps for the Sega Saturn include checking the power supply, checking the cables, cleaning the system, checking the system software, checking the game discs, checking the game cartridges, checking the sound card, checking the audio settings, and checking for updates.
