Question: What is the gameplay of Final Fantasy VII?
Answer: Final Fantasy VII's gameplay is a perfect blend of depth and accessibility. The game features a turn-based battle system, where players can choose from a variety of spells, abilities, and weapons to defeat their enemies. The game also introduces the concept of Materia, a magical substance that allows characters to perform powerful spells and abilities. Players can collect and equip different types of Materia to customize their characters and tailor their strategies to each battle. The game also features a vast open world to explore, with many side quests and secrets to discover.

Question: What is the storyline of Final Fantasy VII?
Answer: Final Fantasy VII's storyline is a complex and emotionally resonant tale of heroism, sacrifice, and redemption. The game takes place in a post-apocalyptic world where a powerful corporation known as Shinra has drained the planet's life force to create a powerful energy source known as Mako. The protagonist, Cloud Strife, is a former member of Shinra's elite military unit, SOLDIER, who becomes embroiled in a battle against Shinra and its powerful leaders. The game's story is full of twists and turns, with many unexpected revelations and surprises. The characters are well-developed and complex, with their own motivations and backstories that add depth and emotional resonance to the story. The game also explores themes of environmentalism, corporate greed, and the power of friendship and love.
