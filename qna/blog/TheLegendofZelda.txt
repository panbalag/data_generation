Question: What is the storyline of The Legend of Zelda?
Answer: The storyline of The Legend of Zelda is simple but epic. The kingdom of Hyrule is threatened by the evil Ganon, who has stolen the Triforce of Power, a powerful artifact that gives its holder immense power. Link, the hero of the game, must retrieve the Triforce of Power and defeat Ganon to save Hyrule.
