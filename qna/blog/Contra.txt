Question: What is the storyline of Contra?
Answer: The storyline of Contra is that the alien force has invaded Earth and is threatening to destroy humanity. Players take on the roles of two soldiers, Bill and Lance, who are sent on a mission to fight against the aliens and save the world. The game is divided into six levels, each with its own unique environment and enemies. Players must fight their way through jungles, deserts, and cities, each with their own challenges and obstacles. The final level takes place on a spaceship, where players must face off against the alien leader and defeat it to save the world.
