Question: What is the storyline of Frank and the Wombats?
Answer: The story of Frank and the Wombats is set in a distant future where humanity has spread across the galaxy, but the peace is threatened by an ancient alien race known as the Xarx. The Xarx are awakening from their slumber and are determined to reclaim their lost empire, using their advanced technology to enslave or destroy any who stand in their way. Frank, a skilled engineer and explorer, is recruited by a secret organization known as the Order of the Wombat to travel the galaxy and unlock the ancient machines that can stop the Xarx. With the help of his loyal wombats, Frank must explore the planets, solve puzzles, and defeat the Xarx to save humanity.
