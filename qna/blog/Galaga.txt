Question: What is the gameplay of Galaga?
Answer: The gameplay of Galaga involves controlling a spaceship that can move left and right across the screen and shoot energy bolts at alien spacecraft that appear in the sky. The game has several power-ups that the player can collect to enhance their ship's abilities, such as a laser weapon that can destroy enemies more quickly and a shield that protects the ship from enemy attacks. The game also features a unique "galaga" feature, where the player can capture some of the enemies and use them as temporary shields to protect the ship from other enemies.
